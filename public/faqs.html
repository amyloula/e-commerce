    Orders and Payments

    You can order Noodies online directly from us here, at noodies.io. Simply add your desired products to your shopping cart, click checkout, login to your PayPal account, or enter your payment details and pay. You will receive instant confirmation.

    We process all of our payments through PayPal. This means you can pay using any currency, use your PayPal balance, register any Visa or Mastercard credit or debit card, as well as any PayPal credit you may have.

    In the PayPal checkout process you will see a field asking if you have any discount or promotional codes, please enter your code there. Remember that discount codes are case-sensitive. E.g 'NOODIES' is not the same as 'noodies'.
